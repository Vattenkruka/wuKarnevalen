Michael Jackson Hologram

Michael Joseph Jackson (August 29, 1958 – June 25, 2009) was an American singer, songwriter, and dancer. Dubbed the "King of Pop", he is regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century and one of the greatest entertainers. Jackson's contributions to music, dance, and fashion, along with his publicized personal life, made him a global figure in popular culture for over four decades. Nevertheless, through our combined efforts with our science team on LundaKarnevalen we have managed to develope a hologram of Michael Jackson which will be part of the show!
